Quick Glossary
BOS Break of Structure — price breaks a previous swing high/low, signaling a shift in direction
FVG Fair Value Gap — an imbalance (3-candle pattern) where price moved so fast it left a gap that needs to be filled
IFVG Inverse FVG — a filled FVG that now acts as support/resistance in the opposite direction
SMT Smart Money Technique — when ES and NQ diverge (one makes a new high/low, the other doesn't), signaling a fake-out
Liquidity Resting orders (stops) above swing highs or below swing lows that institutions target before reversing
Sweep Price briefly breaks a key level to grab liquidity (stop orders) then reverses — this is Rule 1
Draw on Liq. The next pool of liquidity in your direction — this is your target/exit point
Equilibrium The 50% midpoint of a price range — price often retraces here before continuing
79% Ext. A 79% Fibonacci extension level — used as a precise entry trigger
Manipulation The 9:30-9:50 AM window where smart money runs stops before the real move begins
Sell-side Liq. Stops sitting below swing lows — swept to fuel long moves
Buy-side Liq. Stops sitting above swing highs — swept to fuel short moves
